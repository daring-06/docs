The openpilot roadmap lays out the goals for the next major release and gives you visibility into what the team is actively working on. Whether you want to track progress on a specific feature, contribute to an open project, or pick up a paid bounty, you can find the right entry point below.

## openpilot 1.0

openpilot 1.0 will feature a fully end-to-end driving policy. The four pillars of this release are:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="End-to-End Longitudinal Control" icon="gauge-high">
    Full end-to-end longitudinal control will ship in **Chill mode**, completing the transition to a unified, learned driving policy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Driver Monitoring with Sleep Detection" icon="eye">
    The driver monitoring system will gain **sleep detection**, making it meaningfully safer for long drives.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rolling Updates via CI" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Releases will be pushed out automatically by CI, giving you **rolling updates** without waiting for manual release cycles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="panda Safety 1.0" icon="shield-halved">
    The panda safety model will reach its 1.0 milestone, establishing a stable and well-defined safety interface.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
